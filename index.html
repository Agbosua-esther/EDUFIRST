Education

Education is the most powerful weapon in fighting poverty.
EDUFIRST provides educational relief materials to children irrespective of who they are,and where and where they live in undeserved communities.





The Challenge

According to UNICEF, about 10.5 million children are out of school due to educational provision in predominantly rural areas. Some of the reason for this high statistics is lack of basic infrastructures,teaching aids,textbooks are responsible







Solution

EDUFIRST partners with donor organizations to solve educational problems in undeserved communities.
